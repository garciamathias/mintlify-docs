## Why Choose Presti?

### Performance and Speed
Generate photorealistic lifestyle scenes in seconds, not days. Our AI model is specifically trained on furniture, ensuring material consistency, realistic shadows, and accurate scaling. Each generation takes only seconds, allowing you to iterate quickly and perfect your image in 5-10 minutes using our editing tools.

### Cost Efficiency
Reduce your visual content costs by up to 90% compared to traditional photoshoots and 3D renders. Eliminate expenses associated with:
- Studio rental and setup
- Professional photography equipment
- Styling and staging costs
- Post-production workflows
- Logistics and transportation

### Built Exclusively for Furniture
Unlike generic AI tools, Presti is purpose-built for the furniture industry. Our model is trained on a vast dataset of furniture images, understanding the unique challenges of your sector:
- **Materials**: Wood, fabric, leather, metal - rendered perfectly every time
- **Physics**: Realistic shadows and weight that make furniture look real
- **Scale**: Perfect proportions in any room setting
- **Quality**: High-resolution, photorealistic results indistinguishable from real photos
- **Standards**: E-commerce ready, professional results
