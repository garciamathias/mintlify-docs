## Video Creation from Still Images

Convert your generated lifestyle scenes into short, looping videos perfect for digital marketing and social media.

<Info>
  **Available on**: Pro, Team, and Enterprise plans
</Info>

## How Video Generation Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Still Image">
    Generate or select your perfect lifestyle scene
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Video Option">
    Click the video icon in the toolbar
  </Step>
  <Step title="Process">
    AI creates smooth camera movement and subtle animations
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download">
    Export in MP4 format for universal compatibility
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Video Specifications

### Technical Details

| Specification | Details |
|--------------|---------|
| **Resolution** | ~720p (matches source image ratio) |
| **Format** | MP4 (H.264 codec) |
| **Duration** | 3-5 seconds |
| **Loop** | Seamless loop enabled |
| **Frame Rate** | 30 fps |
| **File Size** | 5-15 MB typically |

<Note>
  Video resolution depends on input image. A 1024×1024 image produces a 1024×1024 video (~1M pixels, similar to 720p).
</Note>

## Best Practices

### Source Image Preparation

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Choose high-quality stills">
    Videos inherit the quality of source images. Start with at least 2K resolution for best results.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Consider composition">
    Leave space for camera movement. Centered products with surrounding space work best.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Lighting matters">
    Well-lit scenes produce better videos. Use Relight tool if needed before converting.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Simple scenes often work better">
    Minimalist compositions create more impactful videos than cluttered scenes.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Video Optimization

| Platform | Recommended Settings |
|----------|---------------------|
| **Instagram Feed** | Square (1:1), under 60 seconds |
| **Instagram Stories** | Portrait (9:16), 15 seconds max |
| **Facebook** | Landscape (16:9) or Square |
| **Website Hero** | Landscape (16:9), optimized file size |
