## Brand-Specific AI Training

Your Custom AI is an enterprise feature that fine-tunes Presti.ai's model specifically for your brand, ensuring consistent outputs that match your unique style.

<Note>
  **Availability**: Enterprise plans only. Contact sales for setup.
</Note>

## How Custom AI Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Brand Analysis">
    We analyze your existing visual assets and brand guidelines
  </Step>
  <Step title="Data Collection">
    You provide product catalogs and approved lifestyle imagery
  </Step>
  <Step title="Model Training">
    AI learns your specific style, compositions, and preferences
  </Step>
  <Step title="Validation">
    Test outputs are reviewed and refined with your team
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deployment">
    Custom model becomes available in your workspace
  </Step>
</Steps>
