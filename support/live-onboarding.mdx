## Weekly Live Training

Get the most out of Presti.ai with our expert-led onboarding sessions. Learn best practices, discover hidden features, and get your questions answered in real-time.

<Info>
  **Schedule**: Every Thursday at 2:00 PM CET (Paris time)
  **Duration**: 45 minutes + 15 minutes Q&A
  **Format**: Small group (maximum 10 participants)
  **Cost**: FREE for all users
</Info>

## How to Register

### Registration Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Dashboard">
    Log into your Presti.ai account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Training Section">
    Navigate to Help to Live Training
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Session">
    Select upcoming date that works for you
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register">
    Click "Register" and confirm your spot
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive Confirmation">
    Get calendar invite with joining details
  </Step>
</Steps>
