## Technical

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What formats are supported?">
    JPG, PNG, WebP. Max 50MB. Min 1000×1000px.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Product appears floating?">
    Move product lower on canvas to establish floor contact.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Perspective issues?">
    All products must be shot from same angle. AI cannot rotate.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Which browsers work?">
    Latest Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
