## Before Contacting Support

### Quick Self-Help

1. **Check Documentation**: Search this knowledge base
2. **Review FAQ**: Common questions answered
3. **Try Troubleshooting**: Follow our guides
4. **Watch Tutorials**: Video demonstrations

### Gather Information

To help us assist you faster, please provide:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="For Technical Issues">
    - **Account email**
    - **Workspace/Project name**
    - **Screenshots** of the issue
    - **Steps to reproduce**
    - **Error messages** (exact text)
    - **Browser** and version
    - **Time** issue occurred
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="For Account Issues">
    - **Account email**
    - **Subscription type**
    - **Billing concerns**
    - **Team member info** (if applicable)
    - **Specific question**
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="For Feature Requests">
    - **Use case description**
    - **Current workflow**
    - **Desired outcome**
    - **Business impact**
    - **Priority level**
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Response Guidelines

### What to Expect

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Initial Response">
    - Acknowledgment within 24 hours
    - Ticket number provided
    - Next steps outlined
    - Timeline estimate given
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Issue Resolution">
    - Clear communication throughout
    - Step-by-step guidance
    - Screenshots when helpful
    - Follow-up to ensure resolution
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Escalation Process">
    For complex issues:
    - Technical team involvement
    - Priority handling available
    - Direct specialist contact
    - Executive escalation if needed
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Support Tiers

### By Plan Level

| Plan | Support Level | Response Time | Channels |
|------|---------------|---------------|----------|

| **Pro** | Standard | 24-48 hours | Email + Chat |

## Live Onboarding Sessions

### Weekly Group Training

<Info>
  **When**: Every Thursday at 2 PM CET
  **Duration**: 45 minutes + Q&A
  **Format**: Small group (max 10)
  **Language**: English (French available)
</Info>

### What's Covered

- Platform overview
- Best practices demonstration
- Common workflows
- Tips and tricks
- Live Q&A
- Networking with other users

### How to Join

1. Available to all plan levels
2. Register via your dashboard
3. Receive calendar invite
4. Join via provided link
5. Bring your questions!

## Emergency Support

### Critical Issues

For urgent production-stopping issues:

1. **Email**: support@presti.ai with "URGENT" in subject
2. **Include**: Clear problem description
3. **Provide**: Business impact statement
4. **Expect**: Expedited response

<Warning>
  Please reserve urgent tags for true emergencies that prevent business operations.
</Warning>
