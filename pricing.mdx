## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I change plans anytime?">
    Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll receive additional credits immediately. When downgrading, your remaining credits will carry over.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens if I run out of credits?">
    You can purchase additional credit packs or upgrade to a higher plan. All unused credits roll over to the next billing period.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial for paid plans?">
    We offer a Free plan with 3 credits so you can test all our features before committing to a paid plan.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do you offer discounts for annual billing?">
    Yes, all our paid plans (Core, Pro, Team) are billed annually with significant savings compared to monthly billing.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
