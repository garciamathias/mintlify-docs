## Why Choose Presti.ai?

### Performance and Speed
Generate photorealistic lifestyle scenes in seconds, not days. Our AI model is specifically trained on furniture, ensuring material consistency, realistic shadows, and accurate scaling. Average generation time is 5-10 seconds per image, enabling rapid iteration and testing of multiple concepts.

### Cost Efficiency
Reduce your visual content costs by up to 90% compared to traditional photoshoots and 3D renders. Eliminate expenses associated with:
- Studio rental and setup
- Professional photography equipment
- Styling and staging costs
- Post-production workflows
- Logistics and transportation

### Industry-Specific Design
Our proprietary model is trained exclusively on furniture datasets, providing deep understanding of:
- Material properties and rendering (wood grain, fabric textures, metal finishes)
- Physically accurate shadow generation and lighting
- Proportional scaling and spatial relationships
- Context-appropriate placement and staging
- Industry-standard composition rules

### Measurable Business Impact
- **300+ furniture brands** actively using the platform
- **10x faster** time-to-market for new product launches
- **90% reduction** in visual content production costs
- **85% decrease** in product photography turnaround time
- **3x increase** in visual asset variety per SKU
