## Multi-Product Scene Creation

Stage multiple products together to showcase complete room solutions and product families.

<Info>
  **Available on**: Team and Enterprise plans
</Info>

## Critical Success Factor

<Warning>
  **#1 Rule**: All products MUST be photographed from the same angle. The canvas cannot rotate items.
</Warning>

### Why Perspective Matters

When perspectives don't match:
- Products look disconnected
- Scene appears unrealistic  
- Professional quality suffers
- Customers lose trust

## Pre-Production Planning

### Photography Alignment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Standard Angle">
    Select one consistent viewing angle for all products (e.g., 45° front-left)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mark Camera Position">
    Use tape or markers to ensure consistent placement
  </Step>
  <Step title="Document Settings">
    Record height, distance, and angle for repeatability
  </Step>
  <Step title="Batch Shoot">
    Photograph all products in one session when possible
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Angle Reference Guide

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Standard 45°">
    **Most Versatile**
    - Shows front and side
    - Works for most furniture
    - Natural viewing angle
    - Easy to match
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Front Facing">
    **Symmetrical Items**
    - Best for sofas, beds
    - Shows full width
    - Formal presentation
    - Limited depth info
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="3/4 View">
    **Dynamic Angle**
    - Maximum dimension visibility
    - Shows all surfaces
    - More complex to match
    - Professional look
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Scale & Proportion

### Manual Scaling Tips

<Warning>
  AI doesn't know actual dimensions — you must scale items relative to each other.
</Warning>

**Reference Standards:**
- Sofa seat: ~18" high
- Dining table: ~30" high
- Coffee table: ~16-18" high
- Desk: ~29-30" high

### Visual Scale Checking

| Check | What to Look For |
|-------|-------------------|
| **Height Relationships** | Coffee table lower than sofa seat |
| **Proportion** | Chairs fit under dining table |
| **Spacing** | Realistic walking paths |
| **Context** | Items sized for room scale |

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Perspective Mismatch">
    **Symptoms:** Items look wrong together
    **Solution:** Re-shoot with matching angles
    **Prevention:** Standardize photography
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Scale Problems">
    **Symptoms:** Unrealistic proportions
    **Solution:** Research actual dimensions
    **Prevention:** Create scale reference guide
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Cluttered Scene">
    **Symptoms:** Too busy, no focus
    **Solution:** Remove items, increase spacing
    **Prevention:** Start minimal, add gradually
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Lighting Conflicts">
    **Symptoms:** Different shadow directions
    **Solution:** Use consistent Relight setting
    **Prevention:** Match original photo lighting
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
