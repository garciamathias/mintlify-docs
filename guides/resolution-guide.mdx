## Detailed Resolution Breakdown

### 1K (1280 × 720) — Quick & Lightweight

**Best For:**
- Mobile-first web galleries
- Social stories & reels
- Email thumbnails/previews
- Compressed e-commerce images
- Quick client previews
- Internal reviews

**Print Viability:**
- Not recommended for professional print
- Small tags/icons at 72-100 DPI only
- Maximum print size: 4" × 3" at low quality

**Why Choose 1K?**
- Smallest files (0.5-2 MB)
- Fastest load times
- Lowest credit cost
- Perfect for digital-only use

### 2K (1920 × 1080) — Standard HD

**Best For:**
- Web galleries & hero banners
- Lifestyle shots in ads
- Medium digital signage
- Marketplaces (Wayfair, Amazon)
- Standard e-commerce
- Email campaigns

**Print Viability:**
- Up to 6-8 inches wide at 300 DPI
- Brochures, small POS materials
- Half-page magazine ads
- Product cards

**Why Choose 2K?**
- Balanced quality vs. load time
- Your everyday workhorse
- Sharp on all screens
- Cost-effective

### 4K (3840 × 2160) — Professional-Grade Detail

**Best For:**
- Zoom-in product viewers
- Room scene showcases
- Print catalogs & mailers
- Premium product pages
- Trade show displays
- Large format web

**Print Viability:**
- Up to 13 inches (33 cm) wide at 300 DPI
- Full-page magazine spreads
- High-quality brochures
- Posters up to 20" at 200 DPI

**Why Choose 4K?**
- Crisp zoom functionality
- Print-ready quality
- Future-proof assets
- Professional standard

### 8K (7680 × 4320) — Ultra-Luxury & Large Format

**Best For:**
- Wall prints & window displays
- Showroom backdrops
- Coffee-table catalogs
- AR/VR experiences
- Billboard advertising
- Flagship store displays

**Print Viability:**
- Up to 25 inches (63 cm) wide at 300 DPI
- Up to 60 inches (152 cm) at 150 DPI
- Museum-quality prints
- Large format anything

**Why Choose 8K?**
- Maximum detail capture
- Unmatched depth
- Luxury presentation
- Archive quality

## Understanding DPI for Print

### DPI Quick Reference

Resolution × DPI determines maximum print size:

| Resolution | 300 DPI (Gallery) | 200 DPI (Standard) | 150 DPI (Large) | 72 DPI (Web) |
|------------|-------------------|--------------------|--------------------|---------------|
| **1K** | 4.3" × 2.4" | 6.4" × 3.6" | 8.5" × 4.8" | 17.8" × 10" |
| **2K** | 6.4" × 3.6" | 9.6" × 5.4" | 12.8" × 7.2" | 26.7" × 15" |
| **4K** | 12.8" × 7.2" | 19.2" × 10.8" | 25.6" × 14.4" | 53.3" × 30" |
| **8K** | 25.6" × 14.4" | 38.4" × 21.6" | 51.2" × 28.8" | 106.7" × 60" |
